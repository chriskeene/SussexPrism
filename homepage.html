Advance preview: try out the next generation of our library catalogue

This site is a early preview of the new design of our catalogue. In September this new design will take the place of the existing catalogue and the old 'Standard Catalogue'.

The new catalogue is still under active development, and new features will be added constantly between now and September.

We're very interesting in any comments you have on the work so far, let us know using the online feedback form or using the email address: lib-feedback@sussex.ac.uk.
