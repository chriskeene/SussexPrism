University of Sussex Library Catalogue

From the summer of 2012 this catalogue takes the place of our previous catalogue and the old 'Standard Catalogue'.

The new catalogue is still under active development, and new features will be added between now and September.

In the meantime, please feel free to try it out: it holds up-to-the-minute information about items and loans, and will allow you to manage your borrower account.

If you have any comments, let us know at lib-feedback@sussex.ac.uk
